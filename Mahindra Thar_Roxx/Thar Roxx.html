THE INDIAN OFF ROADER IS BACK!

Mahindra has finally launched the much-awaited second-gen Thar Roxx. The iconic Thar is born again in an all-new avatar. The much-anticipated Thar Roxx, a new evolution of the iconic Thar. Thar Roxx retains the rugged DNA that enthusiasts love, while stepping up in style and technology. With a bold design, enhanced off-road prowess, and refined comfort, Thar Roxx is engineered to conquer both urban roads and challenging terrains. It comes equipped with a powerful engine lineup, including a 2.0-litre petrol and a diesel option, paired with both automatic and manual transmissions. Experience ultimate freedom with versatile roof choices—Hard Top, convertible Soft Top, and Soft Top. Thar Roxx offers the perfect blend of adventure and luxury, ready to take on any challenge!

The AX variant of Thar comes with the following features as standard:

  • Fixed Soft Top
  • 6-seater configuration (2 Front + 5 Side-Facing)
  • R16 White Steel Wheels
  • Steel Footsteps
  • Mechanical Locking Differential
  • Power Steering with power windows
  • HAVC
  • Dual Airbags with ABS
  • Central Locking
  • Intellipark: Rear Parking Assist

Engine & Transmission Option on Offer

  • Diesel engine is available with Manual transmission
  • Petrol engine is available with Manual transmission

The LX variant of Thar comes with the following features as standard:

  • Convertible or Hard Top Option
  • 4-seater
  • Dual Tone Bumpers
  • Moulded Footsteps
  • R18 Alloy Wheels with 255/65 tires
  • LED DRLs with Front Fog Lamps
  • Height and Lumbar Adjustment on Front Seats
  • Premium Fabric Upholstery
  • Touch Screen Infotainment + TFT MID Screen
  • Tyretronics & Tire Direction Monitoring System
  • Electronic Stability Program (ESP)
  • Electronic HVAC Switches
  • Brake Locking Differential

Talking about the performance parts, the LX variant gets:

  • Diesel with Manual or Automatic Transmission
  • Petrol with Automatic Transmission

There are a few optional accessories:

  • Convertible or Hard Top
  • Front facing rear seats with roll cage
  • Remote Keyless Entry only available with Hard Top
  • Height Adjustable Driver Seat

Mahindra thar is available in both petrol and Diesel engine options which have been upgraded.

  • The petrol engine on offer is a mStallion GDI Engine that produces 112kW(~150 BHP) and 320Nm.
  • The diesel engine on offer is a mHawk engine which churns out 97(~130BHP) and 300Nm of torque.

There are two transmissions on offer a 6 Speed Manual Transmission and a 6 Speed torque Converter Automatic transmission. Along with these, is a Shift on the fly 4×4 Manual Shift Transfer case with low, 42:1, crawl ratio.
